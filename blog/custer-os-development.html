Building Custer OS: Our Journey in Operating System Development

Started 6 months ago, we've made significant progress with our first prototype completed within a month. Join us as we share our journey in developing this innovative operating system from scratch.

Introduction

In the world of operating systems, we're breaking new ground with Custer OS - a lightweight, developer-focused operating system that challenges traditional desktop environments. Our journey began 6 months ago with a vision to create something unique for developers who value simplicity and efficiency.

The Vision Behind Custer OS

Unlike traditional operating systems with complex desktop environments, Custer OS takes a minimalist approach. Inspired by the efficiency of Openbox, we've created a streamlined environment that prioritizes coding and development workflows. This focus on simplicity doesn't mean compromising functionality - it means optimizing for what matters most to developers.

Technical Challenges and Solutions

Our biggest challenges came in two main areas:

  • Assembly Language Learning Curve: Developing an operating system requires deep understanding of low-level programming. We spent significant time mastering assembly language to ensure optimal performance and hardware interaction.
  • Debugging Complexities: Operating system development brings unique debugging challenges. We developed custom debugging tools and implemented comprehensive logging systems to track down issues effectively.

First Prototype Features

Within our first month of development, we successfully implemented two core applications:

  • Text Editor: A lightweight, feature-rich editor designed specifically for coding, with syntax highlighting and essential development tools.
  • Web Browser: A streamlined browser optimized for documentation reading and web development.

Future Development

Our roadmap includes expanding the application ecosystem while maintaining our commitment to lightweight, efficient software. We're focusing on:

  • Enhanced development tools integration
  • Package management system
  • Container support for development environments
  • Cross-platform development capabilities

Conclusion

Custer OS represents our commitment to innovation in operating system development. By focusing on developer needs and maintaining a lightweight architecture, we're creating a unique platform that challenges traditional desktop environments while providing powerful development capabilities.
