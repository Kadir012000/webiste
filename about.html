Our Story

Founded on March 31, 2025, General Development emerged from a vision to create innovative software solutions. Based in Vienna, Austria, we are a dynamic team of two passionate individuals dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology.

Our journey began with a commitment to excellence in software development. We started our app development journey 7 months ago, and 6 months ago we embarked on an exciting operating system project called Custer OS, completing our first prototype within a month. Our focus spans across creating custom solutions for Windows PCs, mobile applications for Android and iOS, and game development.
